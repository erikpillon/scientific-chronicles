Ever wondered what makes people tick? Abraham Maslow, the ultimate human potential hype-man, gave us the iconic "Hierarchy of Needs" pyramid. Forget basic survival; Maslow argued we're all climbing towards self-actualization, striving to be our best, most creative selves. He championed humanistic psychology, believing everyone has innate goodness and a drive for growth. So next time you're debating whether to grab a snack or pursue your dreams, remember Maslow's pyramid—it’s all part of the climb!
