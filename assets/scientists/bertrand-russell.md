Meet Bertrand Russell, the kind of philosopher who'd make your brain do backflips! This brilliant Welshman wasn't content until he'd built the very foundations of mathematics, essentially asking, "How do we *know* math works?" He developed modern formal logic and even co-wrote the epic *Principia Mathematica* (yes, it's as intense as it sounds) to prove that math could be built from pure logic.
