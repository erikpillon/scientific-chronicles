Ever listen to someone talk in circles and think, "Literally none of those words mean anything"? Rudolf Carnap made an entire career out of that feeling. 🤔📏

He was a leading figure in Logical Positivism. Basically, Carnap believed that if a statement couldn't be proven through strict logic or empirical science, it was just pure nonsense. He wanted to clean up philosophy by treating language like a rigorous mathematical system. If you couldn't define your terms perfectly, he didn't want to hear it. He was essentially the ultimate debate-bro, but armed with terrifyingly complex symbolic logic.
