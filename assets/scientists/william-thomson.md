William Thomson, aka Lord Kelvin, was such a big deal they called him the "Newton of his era"! Talk about high praise. 🤩 This brilliant physicist gave us the Kelvin temperature scale, which isn't just for science nerds; it's the absolute zero scale, meaning no more negative temps! ❄️ But he wasn't just stuck in the lab; he was a practical genius too, playing a *massive* role in making the transatlantic telegraph cable actually work. Imagine sending messages across the ocean instantly for the first time! He combined pure science with incredible engineering. What a legend! 🌊
