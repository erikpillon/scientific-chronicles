Ever notice how hurricanes spin, or why a tossed ball seems to curve differently on a merry-go-round? That's the Coriolis effect doing its thing!🌪️ And we've got Gaspard-Gustave Coriolis to thank for figuring it out. This French engineer got to the bottom of those "extra" forces you feel in a rotating system. It's not just about weather; it's about physics being kinda sneaky! 😉
