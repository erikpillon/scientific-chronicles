Have you ever wondered about the probability of *that* happening? 🤔 Chances are, Siméon-Denis Poisson probably had an equation for it! This French mathematician gave us the famous 'Poisson distribution,' which is super handy for predicting rare events (like how many meteor showers you might see in a year, or how many calls a call center gets in an hour). But he wasn't just a probability wizard; he also dove deep into electromagnetic theory, potential theory, and even figured out some cool stuff about how planets move. A true master of many scientific trades!
