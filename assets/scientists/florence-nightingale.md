Everyone knows Florence Nightingale as the "Lady with the Lamp" who pioneered modern nursing during the Crimean War. But here is the really cool part: she was an absolute savage with statistics. 📊🏥

When she got to the military hospitals, she realized soldiers weren't dying from battle wounds—they were dying from raw sewage and horrific hygiene. The military brass didn't want to listen to her. So, she invented a new type of data visualization, the polar area diagram (a super-powered pie chart), to brutally lay out the death tolls. The visual was so clear and terrifying that the government had no choice but to fix the sanitation. She literally used infographics to force systemic change.
