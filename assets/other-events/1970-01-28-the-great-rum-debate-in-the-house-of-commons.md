On this day in 1970, the issue of the Royal Navy's daily rum allowance was raised in the House of Commons, becoming known as 'The Great Rum Debate'. The debate concluded that rum made sailors drunk and irresponsible, leading to the discontinuation of the daily ration on 31 July 1970.
