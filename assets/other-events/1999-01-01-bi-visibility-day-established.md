Bi-Visibility Day, also known as International Celebrate Bisexuality Day, was established by Wendy Curry, Michael Page and Gigi Raven Wilbur and first recognised in 1999. Since then, on 23rd September, events take place globally to raise awareness and call for positive change for the bisexual community.
