Archduke Franz Ferdinand, heir to the Austro-Hungarian empire, was assassinated in Sarajevo. After escaping an initial bomb attack, he was shot by Gavrilo Princip while visiting his injured entourage in the hospital. This event is considered the first step on the road to worldwide war.
