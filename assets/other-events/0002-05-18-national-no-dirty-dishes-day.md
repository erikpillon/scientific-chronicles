Probably created by a fed-up and overloaded homemaker, No Dirty Dishes Day is a day dedicated to keeping our sinks clear of dirty plates, pots and pans. But it represents more than just having a clean and tidy sink area and encourages us all to take a break from our daily chores, to marvel at our gleaming basins and to relax and have some fun instead.
