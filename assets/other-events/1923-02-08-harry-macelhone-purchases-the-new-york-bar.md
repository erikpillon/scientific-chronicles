Harry MacElhone bought the New York Bar on 5 Rue Daunou, Paris, where he had worked for over a decade. The bar, later renamed Harry's New York Bar, is said to be 'mainland Europe's oldest, still operating cocktail bar' and has remained in the same family for three generations.
