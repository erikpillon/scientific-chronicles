María Eva Duarte de Perón, known as Evita, died aged 33. She rose from B-movie acting to become First Lady of Argentina, potentially influencing women's right to vote and establishing a foundation to reduce poverty. Her body was mummified and later buried in a secure tomb in Buenos Aires.
