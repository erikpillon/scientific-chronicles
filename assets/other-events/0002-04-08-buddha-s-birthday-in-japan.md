The Japanese, using the standard Western calendar, celebrate Buddha's birthday on April 8th. At Hana-Matsuri, or Flower Festival, shrines are decorated with flowers to commemorate the birth of Siddhartha Gautama, who would later become the Buddha, more than two thousand years ago. Worshippers sprinkle baby Buddhas with sweet tea.
