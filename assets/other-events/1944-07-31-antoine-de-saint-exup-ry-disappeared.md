On this day in 1944, Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, author of The Little Prince and a pioneer aviator, disappeared while flying his ninth reconnaissance mission from an airbase on Corsica in an unarmed P-38. The remains of his plane were not discovered until more than 50 years later after a fisherman found his silver identity bracelet off the coast of Marseille.
