Oceans cover over 70% of the earth's surface, with 95% remaining unexplored. Marine species are dying off faster than they have in 55 million years, and 80% of pollutants in the oceans derive from human activity. Plastic is found in the stomachs of various creatures, and global seafood fondness leads to over 12 million tonnes of marine life taken by China alone annually, with predictions of fish stock collapse before the second half of this century.
