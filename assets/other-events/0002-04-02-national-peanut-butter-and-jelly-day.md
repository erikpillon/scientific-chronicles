April 2nd is officially "National Peanut Butter and Jelly Day". The average American will have eaten over 2,000 peanut butter and jelly sandwiches by the time they graduate from high school. "Jelly" is the American name for jam/conserve and is popularly spread with peanut butter as a sandwich filling. During World War II, peanut butter and jelly were part of American soldiers' rations.
