Pablo Picasso was born this day in 1881. Picasso shares his birthday with International Artist Day, and we can think of nobody better to represent art in so many guises - be it cubism, surrealism, modernism, symbolism and so much more. In fact, it's rumoured that Picasso's first word was 'lapiz' (Spanish for 'pencil')!
