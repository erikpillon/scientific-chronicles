Janis Joplin, one of the first rock stars to form the 27 Club, was born in Texas. Her performances at Monterey, Woodstock, and aboard the Festival Express train made her a counter-culture icon. She was immortalized by Leonard Cohen who wrote Chelsea Hotel for her.
