On this day, protestors tore down the Berlin Wall, which had divided communist East Germany from capitalist West Germany since 1961. The wall was a potent symbol of Communist power, and its fall was a significant moment for Germany and the world, signaling the beginning of a new era as the Iron Curtain fell.
