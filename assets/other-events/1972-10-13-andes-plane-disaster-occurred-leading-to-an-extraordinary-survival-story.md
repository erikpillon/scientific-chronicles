On this day in 1972, a plane carrying 48 people disappeared over the Chilean Andes. Officials abandoned the search after eight days. The survivors, huddled at 12,000 feet, eventually resorted to cannibalism after exhausting airline food. An avalanche later killed nine more. Three strongest survivors walked out for help, and 72 days after the crash, 16 survivors were rescued in mid-December.
