It is the birthday of actor Tom Hanks, known for movies such as Forrest Gump, Saving Private Ryan, Cast Away, and the Toy Story series. In 2014, he was recorded as the highest-grossing actor in film history, and Asteroid 12818 Tomhanks was named after him.
