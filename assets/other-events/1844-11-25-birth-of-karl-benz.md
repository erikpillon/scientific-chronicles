Karl Friedrich Michael Vaillant, later Karl Benz, was born in Karlsruhe, Germany. He went on to design a 'horseless carriage', founded Mercedes-Benz, and invented an engine design still used today. He patented the world's first commercially available automobile, the three-wheeled Patent Motorwagen, in 1886.
