The world's stock markets crashed, with the Dow Jones dropping by over 22%, the greatest percentage loss of all time. By the end of the month, the Hong Kong stock market had almost halved in value, Australia was down over 40%, and British stocks were worth less than three-quarters what they had been just two weeks earlier.
