A day to raise awareness of, and to fight against, the global catastrophe that is single-use plastic pollution. Plastic bags were introduced in the mid-1960s. In 1997, sailor and researcher Charles Moore discovered the Great Pacific Garbage Patch. In 2019, single-use plastic particles were found in the Mariana Trench.
