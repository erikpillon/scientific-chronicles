The 9th of June is an annual public holiday in the autonomous community of La Rioja in northeast Spain, celebrating the anniversary of King Juan Carlos I's signature of the Statute of Autonomy of La Rioja in 1982. La Rioja became a self-administrated province in 1833, originally under the name Logro
00f1o, and was renamed La Rioja in 1980.
