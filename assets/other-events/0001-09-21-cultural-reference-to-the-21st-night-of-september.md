The date is famously referenced in the lyrics of Earth, Wind & Fire's song 'September', released in 1978, which reached No. 1 on the US Billboard Hot R&B Songs chart, No. 8 on the US Billboard Hot 100, and No. 3 on the UK Singles Chart.
