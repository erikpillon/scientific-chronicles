The Allies celebrated victory over Japan, following the Japanese government's unconditional surrender announcement on August 14, 1945, which ended the Second World War. The 15th of August 1945 coincided with the state opening of the British Parliament, marked by public celebrations in the UK, while Japan reeled from the devastation of Hiroshima and Nagasaki.
