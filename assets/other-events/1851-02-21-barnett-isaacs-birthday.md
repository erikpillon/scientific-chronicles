Barnett Isaacs was born in 1852 in the Whitechapel slums of London. He became a successful entrepreneur, making a fortune in the South African diamond rush, and later sold his company to Cecil John Rhodes. He died dramatically in 1897, disappearing overboard while sailing back to England.
