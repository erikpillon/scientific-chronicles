A day to celebrate pastries. The humble pastry has its roots in ancient and classical times, with Egyptians, Romans, and Greeks using filo-type pastry. Shortcrust and puff versions became familiar in the Mediaeval period, and in the 17th century, pastry became extremely fashionable.
