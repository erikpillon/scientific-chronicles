Michael Jackson, also known as The Beer Hunter, was born on this day in 1942. He was a trailblazer in the beer world, publishing his 'World Guide to Beer' in 1977, which set the foundation for the global theory of beer styles and helped spark interest in beer as a varied and interesting liquid, spreading the craft-brewing movement.
