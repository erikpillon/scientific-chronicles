World Art Day celebrates what is an integral part of every culture all over the world and shows appreciation for the greats of the art world, whilst hoping to inspire the inner artist in everyone. April 15th was chosen to mark World Art Day to honour Leonardo da Vinci's birthday and was declared in 2012 at the General Assembly of the International Association of Art, which is also supported by UNESCO.
