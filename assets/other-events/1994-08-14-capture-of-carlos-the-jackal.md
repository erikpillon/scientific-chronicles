Ilich Ramírez Sánchez, the Marxist-Leninist terrorist known as 'Carlos the Jackal', was captured in Sudan. He was one of the world's most wanted men, responsible for assassinations, a bombing campaign, and hostage-taking. He was later sentenced to two life sentences.
