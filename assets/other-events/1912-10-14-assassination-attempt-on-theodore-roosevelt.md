John Schrank attempted to kill ex-president Theodore Roosevelt. The murder attempt was apparently motivated by a dream in which William McKinley asked him to avenge his death. Schrank's bullet hit Roosevelt, but the politician was protected by a steel spectacles case and a 50-page copy of his speech. Roosevelt carried the bullet inside him until he died, and Schrank spent the rest of his life in an insane asylum.
