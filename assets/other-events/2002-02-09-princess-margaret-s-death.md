Princess Margaret died after a life dominated by an awe-inspiring consumption of nicotine and alcohol. During and after her marriage to the photographer Anthony Armstrong-Jones she was an icon of the British 60s, hanging out with friends including Mick Jagger and Peter Sellers, and partying like a demon on the private island of Mustique.
