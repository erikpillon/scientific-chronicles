Half a million people gathered at a farm in upstate New York for the Woodstock music festival, billed as "an Aquarian exposition: 3 days of peace and music." Artists like The Who, Janis Joplin, Joan Baez, Sly & the Family Stone, Jimi Hendrix, and the Grateful Dead performed.
