The Winton Motor Carriage Company placed the world's first car advertisement in Scientific American, suggesting readers 'dispense with a horse'. The ad was successful, leading to sales of over 20 vehicles, including one to James Ward Packard, who later founded his own car company. Winton's motor carriage cost $1000 and reached 20mph.
