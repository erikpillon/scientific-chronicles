A day launched in 2015 by Ranjit S. Baxi to raise awareness around the importance of recycling, its impact on climate change, and the well-being of the planet. March 18th was chosen as it is also the birthday of the Bureau of International Recycling, and it has been recognised by the U.N. since 2018.
