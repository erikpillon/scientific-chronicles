Edgar Allan Poe, the Massachusetts-born poet, was born on this day. Known for his gloomy works like 'The Raven', he was abandoned by his father, orphaned, and widowed. His poetry is loved worldwide, and an anonymous 'Poe Toaster' traditionally visits his grave annually.
