On this day in 2004, HMS Scylla, a navy frigate, was deliberately sunk off the coast of Cornwall. After being carefully cleaned to remove pollutants, HMS Scylla became Europe's first artificial reef, now attracting divers and scientists who have found over 230 species living there.
