Festivities are held on November 1st (All Saints' Day) and November 2nd (All Souls Day), a celebration which (almost) seamlessly blends Christian beliefs with activities so firmly pre-Christian the Aztecs would probably recognize them. Traditionally, today honours deceased children and tomorrow honours adults who have passed away.
