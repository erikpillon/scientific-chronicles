Maize Day, celebrating corn, was inadvertently started in 2004 by artist Corrine Lightweaver and her family, who observed Thanksgiving from the standpoint of indigenous peoples. Corn itself was first domesticated by indigenous peoples of southern Mexico about ten thousand years ago.
