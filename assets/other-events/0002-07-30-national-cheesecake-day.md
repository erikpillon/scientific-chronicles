National Cheesecake Day is celebrated. The dessert is thought to have been conceived by the Ancient Greeks, first appearing in a book by physician Aegimus, and fed to Greek athletes during the Olympics in 776BC. The recipe has since gone global, adapted to local tastes, and remains popular in the United States.
