Hans Christian Andersen, creator of The Little Match Girl, The Little Mermaid, The Princess and the Pea, and The Snow Queen (which inspired Disney's Frozen), was born in Odense, Denmark. He based The Ugly Duckling on his own experiences. Besides fairy tales, he also wrote poems, plays, novels, and travelogues.
