On September 30, 1955, the iconic 1950s actor James Dean died at the age of 24 in a car collision. He was driving his new Porsche Spyder to a race meeting when an oncoming car veered into his lane. Dean was pronounced dead on arrival at the hospital, four years into a meteoric career with roles in 'Rebel without a Cause', 'Giant', and 'East of Eden'.
