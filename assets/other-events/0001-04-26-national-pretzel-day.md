This day celebrates pretzels. In 2002, President George W Bush choked on a pretzel, leading to a fall and injury. According to historians/urban myth, the pretzel is the oldest snack food, first made in 610AD by a monk in southern France or northern Italy. He formed the dough into a shape resembling a child's arms folded in prayer, giving them as rewards to children who learned their prayers. The name 'pretzel' is said to derive from 'pretiola', Latin for 'little reward'.
