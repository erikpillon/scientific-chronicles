King Charles III was crowned at Westminster Abbey. Born on November 14th 1948, Charles Philip Arthur George is the eldest son of the late Queen Elizabeth II and Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh. He held the title of Prince of Wales from 1958 and has been an active member of the British Royal Family, involved in charitable and environmental causes.
