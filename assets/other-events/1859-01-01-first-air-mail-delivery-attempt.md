Balloonist John Wise attempted the first ever mail delivery by air, taking to the skies in Lafayette, Indiana, and aiming for New York with a bag of 123 letters. He landed 30 miles away after a five-hour flight, having dropped the mail ahead of him on a homemade parachute. Wise continued flying, researching, and writing on ballooning until he disappeared over Lake Michigan at age 71.
