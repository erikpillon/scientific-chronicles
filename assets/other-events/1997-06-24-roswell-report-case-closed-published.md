On this day in 1997, the US Air Force released a report on the Roswell UFO rumours, entitled "Roswell Report: Case Closed". The Air Force report found that the "alien bodies" were air force personnel, killed or disfigured by high-altitude accidents, or crash test dummies dropped from high-altitude balloons. The spacecraft were high altitude balloons and the UFOs space probes.
