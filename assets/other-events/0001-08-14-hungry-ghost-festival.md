In China and beyond, the Hungry Ghost Festival is celebrated. According to Buddhism, ancestral spirits return from the grave to visit the living. Traditions include making offerings of food and burning fake banknotes. In Malaysia and Singapore, the day also features live shows, traditional music, and dances.
