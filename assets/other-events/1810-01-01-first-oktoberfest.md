The first Oktoberfest, celebrating the wedding of the crown prince of Bavaria (who became King Louis I) and Princess Therese von Sachsen-Hildburghausen, was held in Munich, Germany. City officials invited members of the public to celebrate the marriage on fields outside the city walls, with a horse race as the main attraction. This annual gathering is the origin of the Oktoberfest tradition.
