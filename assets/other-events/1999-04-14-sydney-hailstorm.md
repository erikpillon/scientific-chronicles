On April 14, 1999, Sydney was hit by one of Australia's most expensive natural disasters: a hailstorm. The storm dumped around 500,000 tonnes of hailstones, some up to 9cm across, damaging approximately 70,000 vehicles and 24,000 houses, causing over AU$2 Billion in damages.
