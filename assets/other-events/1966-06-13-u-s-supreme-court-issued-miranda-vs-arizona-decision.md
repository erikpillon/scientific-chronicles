The U.S. Supreme Court issued its landmark Miranda vs. Arizona decision, ruling that criminal suspects must be informed of their constitutional rights prior to questioning by police. 'You have the right to remain silent. Anything you say may be taken down as evidence and used against you in a court of law...'
