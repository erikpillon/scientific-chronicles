Festivus, an alternative ritual established by Frank Constanza in a Seinfeld episode, reached the public domain. It is based on the real-life family tradition of one of the show's writers, Dan O'Keeffe, and features an aluminium pole, an "Airing of the Grievances," and "Feats of Strengths."
