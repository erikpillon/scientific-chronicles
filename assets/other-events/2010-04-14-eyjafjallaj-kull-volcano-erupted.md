On April 14, 2010, the Eyjafjallajökull volcano in Iceland erupted, sending a plume of ash up to 30,000 feet and causing widespread flight disruption across Europe, the most significant since World War II. London's Heathrow Airport was shut down, and many travelers were stranded.
