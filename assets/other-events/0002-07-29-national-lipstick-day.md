Today is observed as National Lipstick Day. The history of lipstick dates back millennia, with Mesopotamians mixing crushed gemstones and Cleopatra using crushed bugs. Its popularity grew in 16th century England with Queen Elizabeth I, and commercial production began in 1884 when French cosmetics brand Guerlain started manufacturing it.
