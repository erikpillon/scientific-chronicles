On this day in 2012, the Soviet pro basketball player, Alexander Sizonenko, died in hospital in Saint Petersburg, aged 52. He was officially the world's tallest man in 1991 and the world's tallest ever pro basketball player. Sizonenko never stopped growing throughout his life, reaching over 2.5m tall when he died and wearing size 62 shoes. During his life, he took roles in films.
