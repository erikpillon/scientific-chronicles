On this day in 2003, the Liverpudlian soap opera 'Brookside' aired its last ever episode. Known for being cutting-edge, it featured the first pre-watershed lesbian kiss, delved into domestic violence and drug addiction, and featured an out gay man in a non-judgemental fashion.
