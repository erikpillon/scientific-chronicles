Sir Allen Stanford, a financier and founder of Twenty20 cricket, was convicted of operating one of the largest Ponzi schemes ever. He defrauded investors of more than $7 billion by claiming safe investments while losing their money in risky ventures and using new deposits to pay off earlier 'investors'.
