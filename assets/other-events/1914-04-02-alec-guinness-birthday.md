Alec Guinness, one of the best-known British actors of the twentieth century, was born this day in 1914. Best known for Obi-Wan Kenobi in Star Wars, he won an Oscar for his role in The Bridge on the River Kwai and was nominated for a screenwriting Oscar. He also appeared in Lawrence of Arabia, Doctor Zhivago, and A Passage to India.
