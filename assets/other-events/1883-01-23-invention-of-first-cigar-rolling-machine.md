Oscar Hammerstein patented the first cigar-rolling machine. The grandfather of lyricist Oscar Hammerstein II of Rogers & Hammerstein, while a young immigrant he worked sweeping in a cigar factory and progressed through the ranks to eventually edit the United States Tobacco Journal. He used the revenue he received from his patented machine to bankroll opera productions and build theatres.
