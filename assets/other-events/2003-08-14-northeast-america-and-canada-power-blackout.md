A massive power blackout, thought to have started by a power plant in Ohio shutting down, affected over 50 million people across the eastern United States and Canada, crippling major cities like New York and Ottawa. It was the worst power cut in US history, causing widespread chaos including traffic gridlock, railway stoppages, and suspended flights.
