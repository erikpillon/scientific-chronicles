May 2nd is celebrated as International Harry Potter Day, marking the date of the Battle of Hogwarts where Harry Potter defeated Lord Voldemort. In 2012, UK Prime Minister David Cameron announced May 2nd as an international day of celebration for J.K. Rowling's literary contributions.
