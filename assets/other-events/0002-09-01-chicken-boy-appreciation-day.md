This unofficial national day celebrates Chicken Boy, a 6.7-meter (22-foot) tall fibreglass statue on Route 66, described as 'the Statue of Liberty of Los Angeles'. Named after a 1960s restaurant, it received a Governor's Historic Preservation Award in 2010 from Arnold Schwarzenegger.
