On March 31, 1912, workmen in Belfast finished fitting out the RMS Titanic, the largest and most luxurious ocean liner in the world. It was considered "virtually unsinkable" due to its watertight compartments and featured lavish interiors including a gym, a grand staircase, and opulent suites.
