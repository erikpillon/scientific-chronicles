A day dedicated to celebrating popcorn, the classic cinema snack. Popcorn kernels discovered in 1948 in New Mexico were carbon-dated to be over 5,000 years old. Popcorn was used by Aztecs and Native Americans. The first commercial popcorn machine was created by Charles Cretors in 1885, and microwave popcorn bags appeared about 100 years later.
