A day to celebrate plum pudding, a dessert with roots stretching back hundreds of years to mediaeval England. Made from suet, dried fruit, breadcrumbs, sugar, and spices, it was originally a main course for Christmas dinner and evolved into a sweeter dessert, popularized by Queen Victoria.
