A giant molasses tank exploded in Boston, flattening homes, shops and people, and flooding the neighborhood in a tide of molasses. More than 20 people died, and over 150 were injured. The 10 million litres of molasses formed a lethal, sticky and fast-moving tidal wave, reaching around eight metres high and moving at over 30 miles per hour.
