Pharmacist Felix Hoffmann, an employee of Bayer AG, registered Aspirin with Berlin's patent office. Hoffmann's work involved synthesizing and stabilizing salicylic acid, the active ingredient from willow bark, which had been used as a painkiller for over 2000 years.
