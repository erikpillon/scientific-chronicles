Three bank robbers, Frank Lee Morris, Clarence Anglin, and John Anglin, disappeared from Alcatraz, considered the USA's most impregnable prison. They crafted dummy faces, cut through their cells, climbed through a ventilation duct, and made a raft to sail away, never to be seen again.
