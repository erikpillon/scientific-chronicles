Banjo Patterson's epic poem 'The Man from Snowy River' was first published in The Bulletin on this day in 1890. The poem, set northwest of Canberra, captured a distinct 'Australian-ness' and has since inspired movies, a stage musical, a TV show, and a 1971 Slim Dusty recording.
