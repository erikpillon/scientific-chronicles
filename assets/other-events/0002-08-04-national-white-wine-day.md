This day originated in America as a celebration of Californian wine, with the first vineyards established there in the 1850s. The industrious Romans are also thanked for ideas for civilised fun, including drinking chilled white wine in the summer. English white wines have also seen significant improvement in recent years, winning awards at international competitions.
