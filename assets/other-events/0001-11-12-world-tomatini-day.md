World Tomatini Day is held annually on November 12, marking the start of a week of events celebrating the Tomatini cocktail. This signature cocktail was created in 2011 by Jimmy Barrat at La Petite Maison (LPM) restaurant in Dubai and is now served in all LPM restaurants globally.
