Salvatore 'The Beast' Riina, one of the century's most-feared Mob bosses and killer of Judge Giovanni Falcone, was apprehended by carabinieri. He was later convicted of life imprisonment for over one hundred murders, including 17 in a single terrorist-style train bombing. His imprisonment led to a significant decline in the power and violence of the Palermo Mafia.
