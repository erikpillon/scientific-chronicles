The humble pumpkin is the star of the show each 26th of October. It's thought that the pumpkin became associated with Halloween in the 1800s when 'Jack-o'-lanterns' (carved pumpkins lit by candle from within) started appearing in the windows of American homes. Jack-o'-lanterns are said to originate from the old Irish folk tale of Stingy Jack, and the potato famine that saw many Irish immigrants make the journey to America.
