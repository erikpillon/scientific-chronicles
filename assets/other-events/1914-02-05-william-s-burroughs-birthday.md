Poet, author, artist, performer, and leading light of the Beat Generation, William S. Burroughs was born in St. Louis, Missouri, USA. He worked with and influenced artists from Allen Ginsberg to the Beatles, Andy Warhol to Patti Smith. His life included a tragic incident where he accidentally shot his wife.
