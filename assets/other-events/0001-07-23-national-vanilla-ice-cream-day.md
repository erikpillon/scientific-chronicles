National Vanilla Ice Cream Day is celebrated. Vanilla ice cream was introduced to the USA in the 1780s, with Thomas Jefferson bringing a recipe back from France. A copy of his recipe is preserved in the US Library of Congress. In 1841, a new technique for hand-pollinating vanilla was discovered, allowing for its commercial production.
