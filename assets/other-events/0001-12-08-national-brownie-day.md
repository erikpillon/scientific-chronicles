The legend states that the first brownie was created at Chicago's Palmer House Hotel in 1893, when Mrs Palmer requested a small, cake-like dessert for boxed lunches for ladies attending the Chicago World's Columbian Exposition. Modern-style chocolate brownie recipes began appearing in print at the turn of the twentieth century.
