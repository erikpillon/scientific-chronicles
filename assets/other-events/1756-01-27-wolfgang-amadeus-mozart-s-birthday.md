Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart was born on this day in 1756. By age six, he was composing and performing for European royalty. He composed a vast body of work including operas, concertos, quintets, and Requiems before his death at 35, influencing composers like Beethoven, Tchaikovsky, and Chopin.
