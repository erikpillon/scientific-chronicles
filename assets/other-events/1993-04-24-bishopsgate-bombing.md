On this day in 1993, the Bishopsgate Bombing was carried out by the IRA in the City of London. A tonne of fertiliser exploded, killing one person, injuring forty, and causing hundreds of millions of pounds worth of damage, leading to the creation of the 'Ring of Steel'.
