Sir Roger Bannister set a world record for the mile, becoming the first man to run it in under four minutes, covering the distance in 3 minutes 59.4 seconds. Bannister was a medical student who trained in his lunchbreak and at weekends, and later became a pioneering neurologist, academic, and President of the Sports Council of Great Britain.
