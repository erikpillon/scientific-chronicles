The anniversary of the death of Rasputin, the 'Mad Monk' known for his influence over Russian Emperor Nicolas II and his wife. His death was mysterious, involving multiple attempts to kill him, including being shot, poisoned, rolled in a carpet, and thrown into an icy river.
