At midnight on April 6, 1933, beer with a 3.2% alcohol content became legal in 21 states, marking the beginning of the end of Prohibition. President Roosevelt passed the Cullen-Harrison Act, leading to over 1.5 million barrels of beer sold within the first 24 hours.
