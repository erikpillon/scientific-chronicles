This day commemorates the supposed massacre of innocent children by Herod. In Spain, it's known as Día de los Santos Inocentes, a day similar to April Fool's Day, featuring pranks, made-up stories in media, bonfires, and local traditions like flour battles, egg throwing, burning giant paper dolls, and the Dance of the Fools.
