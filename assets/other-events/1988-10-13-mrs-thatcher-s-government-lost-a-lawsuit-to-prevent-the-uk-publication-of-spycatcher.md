In 1988, Mrs Thatcher's government lost a lawsuit attempting to prevent the publication of 'Spycatcher' in the UK. The book, memoirs of a disgruntled ex-MI5 man, had already been published in America and Australia. Its publication significantly impacted MI5, transforming it into a more professional and open organization.
