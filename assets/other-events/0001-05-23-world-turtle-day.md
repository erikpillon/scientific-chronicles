A day dedicated to celebrating and promoting the protection of turtles and tortoises, from sea turtles and terrapins to pet tortoises and their giant cousins. Established in 1990 by American Tortoise Rescue, today is a day for bringing awareness to the disappearing habitat of these beloved reptiles and aims to encourage us all to do what we can to help them both in the wild and in captivity.
