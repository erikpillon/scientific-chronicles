This day celebrates the Italian sweet treat, the macaroon. Its recipe can be traced back to an Italian monastery in the 8th or 9th century, brought to France in 1533, and later popularized by the 'Macaroon Sisters' during the French Revolution. It also became popular in the Jewish community for Passover and is now a classic American cookie.
