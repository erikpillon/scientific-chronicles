James Bond's creator Ian Fleming was born on this day in 1908. He worked in Naval Intelligence during World War II, running commando operations. He published his first Bond novel, Casino Royale, in 1953, and retired to his Jamaican estate Goldeneye to write. A serious film adaptation of Casino Royale was released in 2006, following a 1967 spoof version.
