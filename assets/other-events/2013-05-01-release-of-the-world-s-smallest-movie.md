IBM officially released 'the world's smallest movie' – a stop-motion animation about a little boy and his atom buddy, imaginatively entitled 'A Boy and His Atom'. Scientists made it by individually manipulating individual atoms, with every single frame shot on the atomic scale, and every atom in the picture being a real, bona fide atom.
