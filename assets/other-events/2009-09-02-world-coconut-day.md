World Coconut Day commemorates the foundation of the Asian Pacific Coconut Community (APCC), an intergovernmental organisation that oversees production and promotes coconuts. The APCC was originally formed as the Asian Coconut Community on 9th September 1969, and its name was changed to encompass countries of the Pacific.
