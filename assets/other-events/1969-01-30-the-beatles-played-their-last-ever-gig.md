The Beatles performed their last ever gig on the roof of Apple Records headquarters in Savile Row. This performance was filmed as the closing sequence of their biopic, Let It Be. Cracks in the band's relationships were showing, and though they released 'Abbey Road' later that year, they never performed again, with their split becoming permanent in 1970.
