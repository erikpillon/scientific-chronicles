Today is National Cocoa Day, a day to celebrate chocolate's key ingredient. Cocoa is a highly concentrated powder made from the seeds of the Theobroma Cacao, widely cultivated in the Amazon Basin around 4,000 years ago and used by the Mayans and Aztecs.
