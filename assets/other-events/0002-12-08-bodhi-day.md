On this day every year, Bodhi Day, the Japanese celebrate the enlightenment of Buddha, the moment at which Gautama Siddhartha, while sitting under a bodhi tree, found the solution to the problems he was confronting and achieved Nirvana. In Japan, the festival is known as "rohatsu", or "eighth day of the twelfth month".
