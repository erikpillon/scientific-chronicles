The H-4 Hercules, also known as the Spruce Goose, made its maiden and only flight on this day in 1947. Piloted by Howard Hughes Jr., the aircraft, boasting the largest wingspan of any ever built, flew just over one mile at an altitude of 70 feet for one minute above Long Beach, California.
