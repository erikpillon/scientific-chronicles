November 4th is dedicated to 'candy'. Candy can be dated back to between the 4th and 6th centuries BC when ancient Indians boiled sugarcane juice. It held a major place in cultural history globally, used as medicine before the Industrial Revolution, and became a childhood staple in America by the 18th century.
