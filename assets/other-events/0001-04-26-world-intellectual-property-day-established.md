Observed annually, World Intellectual Property Day was established by the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) in 2000 to raise awareness of intellectual property. The date coincides with the establishment of WIPO in 1970. Intellectual property rights originated with The British Statute of Anne in 1710, initially covering books and written works.
