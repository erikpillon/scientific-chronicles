Rosolio Day, celebrated annually on September 1st, was created by Italicus Rosolio di Bergamotto to honour the brand's 8th anniversary. It is dedicated to celebrating the history of rosolio, an Italian liqueur originally made in convents and monasteries as a medicinal tonic.
