Doctor John Heysham Gibbon successfully used his heart-lung machine for the first time in surgery, removing the heart from an 18-year-old girl and connecting her to the machine, allowing her to live. Gibbon spent almost twenty years designing the machine, which functioned as both heart and lungs, though he later abandoned his research.
