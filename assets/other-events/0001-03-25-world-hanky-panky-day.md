First celebrated on March 25, 2026, World Hanky Panky Day is a tribute to Ada Coleman, who created the Hanky Panky cocktail in the early 1900s. She was the first female head bartender at The Savoy's American Bar, serving a 23-year tenure from 1903 until her retirement in early 1926.
