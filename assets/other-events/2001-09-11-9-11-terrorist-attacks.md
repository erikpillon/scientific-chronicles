Saudi terrorists affiliated with Osama Bin Laden and al Qaeda hijacked four planes on a suicide mission. Two flew into the World Trade Center, one into the Pentagon and one crashed into a field in Shanksville, Pennsylvania. Nearly 3,000 people were killed.
