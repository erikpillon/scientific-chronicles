On this night in 1964, Cassius Clay defeated Sonny Liston to become the World Heavyweight Boxing Champion. The following day, he announced he was changing his name to Cassius X, then a week later to Muhammad Ali. He became a hero of the US civil rights movement and was the first man to win the heavyweight title three times.
