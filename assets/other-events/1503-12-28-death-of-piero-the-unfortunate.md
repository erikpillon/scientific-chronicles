Piero the Unfortunate (also known as Piero the Unlucky), the oldest son of Lorenzo the Magnificent and a member of the Medici dynasty, passed away by drowning in a river after his boat capsized while he was escaping a battle he had lost. He was known for his spoiled, petulant, and arrogant personality.
