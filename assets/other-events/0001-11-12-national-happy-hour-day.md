National Happy Hour Day is celebrated on November 12. The concept of 'happy hour' is said to have originated in America with the US Navy in the early 20th century, as a period for activities to overcome the monotony at sea. The Prohibition-era term 'Cocktail Hour' also influenced its promotion to entice customers into bars earlier.
