Fernand Petiot was a legendary bartender credited with inventing the Bloody Mary. He served drinks to every US President from 1934 to 1972 (except Lyndon Johnson). Petiot began his bartending career in Paris at Harry's New York Bar, moved to the US in 1925, and bartended at the St Regis Hotel bar until his retirement in 1966. He passed away in Canton City, Ohio.
