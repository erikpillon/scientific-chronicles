The town of Lake Havasu City in Arizona debuted a new bridge - London Bridge. Arizonan entrepreneur Robert P. McCulloch had bought the original London Bridge, shipped its individually numbered stones to Arizona, and rebuilt it over a local canal, a project which took four years to complete.
