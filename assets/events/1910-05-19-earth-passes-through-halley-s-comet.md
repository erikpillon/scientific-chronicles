People love a good doomsday panic. In 1910, Earth was scheduled to pass directly through the tail of Halley's Comet. Normally, this would just be a stunning light show. But a few years earlier, astronomers had detected a poisonous gas called cyanogen in the comet. 

The media ran with it. Papers predicted that the gas would sweep through the atmosphere and suffocate everyone on the planet. Astronomers desperately tried to calm everyone down, explaining that the comet's tail was basically a massive vacuum and the gas was too thin to do anything at all. 

Did people listen to the scientists? Of course not. Absolute chaos broke out. Opportunistic scammers made a fortune selling "comet pills" and "anti-comet masks" to terrified folks trying to survive the night. By May 20th, Earth had breezed right through the tail. Everyone woke up completely fine, though definitely a little lighter in the wallet. ☄️💊😂
