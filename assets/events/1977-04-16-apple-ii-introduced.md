The Apple II was Steve Wozniak's masterpiece and Steve Jobs's marketing triumph. It was one of the first personal computers that felt like a consumer product, not a kit for hobbyists. 

It had a plastic case, an integrated keyboard, and it could display color graphics. With the addition of VisiCalc (the first spreadsheet), it became a must-have for businesses too. The Apple II launched a trillion-dollar company and proved that the future of computing was personal. 🍎
